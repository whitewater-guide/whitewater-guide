FEATURES

For now we have implemented only most essential features we believe every kayaker needs. Check out those core features below. There are many cool things we would like to add to our app, like notifications or favourites. But now we are focused on iOS version and on our content.

  • REGION MAP

    Different water levels are indicated with different colors. This allows you to discover which river drainages have perfect flows right now. The map also contains useful points like wild camping spots.

  • ONE-TAP ESSENTIALS

    With one tap you get all the essential information about any section - class, length and current flow. Another tap - and route to put in or take out is displayed in google maps.

  • SECTION MAP

    Section maps feature detailed river shapes and many point of interest, like portages, major rapids, alternative put ins, etc...

  • FLEXIBLE FILTERS

    Only interested in class IV+ runs with waterfalls and no portages? No problem!

  • SECTIONS LIST

    Find sections by names and browse filtered lists to quickly discover all your options.

  • NAVIGATE FROM EVERYWHERE

    We realize that all you really want is to get to the river, so just find the name in the list and tap navigation button.

  • GUIDEBOOKS

    For every section we provide in-depth description and informative table, just like in good old paper guidebook.

  • FLOW CHARTS

    For every river with online gauge we provide water flow or water level chart.

  • MEDIA

    We try to provide informative photos, like photo of the put in, so you can judge the water level even when there is no guide. We also pick videos from the internets, in case you don't enjoy exploring the rivers.
